Common Mistakes When Feeding Raw

  • Did not complete enough research about raw feeding.
  • Quickly rushing through the transition phases.
  • Not adding any raw bones to the diet or feeding too much bones.
  • Providing inappropriate and unsafe bones.
  • Over complicating calculations for feeding amounts.
  • Adding too many supplements to the diet.
  • Not balancing or customizing the diet.

Common Mistakes When Feeding Home Cooked

  • Bad or missing ingredients - nutritionally unbalanced.
  • Not doing enough research.
  • Using bad, untrusted resources when doing research.
  • Feeding cooked bones is really dangerous.
  • Not providing necessary vitamins and minerals.
